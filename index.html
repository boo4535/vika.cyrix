Independence. Safety. Empowerment for the Blind.

VIKA is a revolutionary wearable device designed to transform the lives of blind individuals. It provides 360-degree awareness, advanced safety features, and seamless assistance to ensure independence in daily life.

Join Our Mission

What Makes VIKA Unique?

360° Vision

VIKA's groundbreaking 360-degree vision system combines ultrasonic sensors and advanced AI to provide complete spatial awareness, setting a new standard in assistive technology.

Complete Voice Control

Experience true hands-free operation with VIKA's comprehensive voice control system, allowing users to access all features and receive guidance through natural conversation.

Health Monitoring

VIKA integrates with Oura Ring to provide real-time health tracking, monitoring vital signs and alerting users to any concerning changes.

Emergency Response System

For enhanced safety, VIKA automatically contacts emergency services when needed, sharing critical health data and location information.

Multi-Language Support

VIKA breaks language barriers with support for over 50 languages, making it accessible to users worldwide while maintaining its intuitive voice control interface.

How VIKA Works

01

Detection

Ultrasonic sensors detect obstacles within a 1-2 meter range.

02

Analysis

VIKA's AI-powered system analyzes sensor data and environmental information in real-time to provide users with precise warnings and guidance.

03

Alert

Precise vibrational feedback guides users safely around obstacles.

04

Assistance

VIKA's intelligent assistant provides users with real-time support and guidance, ensuring a seamless and independent experience.
